The Staple Sandwich

Here's the BL-Tea

The BLT is a beloved staple in Western culture, known for its perfect blend of simplicity and flavor. With crispy bacon, fresh lettuce, and juicy tomatoes stacked between slices of toasted bread, it's a timeless favourite. Whether you prefer classic bacon or Canadian, eh, be it crisp and sturdy sourdough or slightly sweet brioche, this classic will have something for everyone. Some prefer adding some sharp cheddar or Monterey jack for extra richness and flavour, though purists may say that's a BLTC. Whether enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner, the BLT continues to be a classic comfort food enjoyed by many, and there’s no limit to the variations you can make to suit your taste.

Give them a B...

And the best part? It's incredibly versatile. You can experiment with different ingredients to elevate your BLT. Try avocado slices for a creamy texture or a fried egg to make it even more indulgent. Feeling adventurous? Add some spicy aioli or sriracha for a kick. You can even switch up the greens with arugula or spinach for a peppery bite. The BLT is as customizable as it is classic, making it a go-to sandwich that can be tailored to any craving. At the heart of it, though, is the simplicity that has made the BLT a household favorite for generations.

...They'll take an LT

When it comes to crafting the perfect BLT, the choice of bread plays a key role in elevating the sandwich. Traditionalists might go for sourdough or whole wheat, preferring that sturdy crunch to balance out the juicy tomatoes. For those craving something more indulgent, croissant bread is an absolute game-changer. Its buttery layers elevate your BLT with that melt-in-your-mouth quality. Brioche is another popular choice, adding a sweetness that pairs beautifully with the savory bacon. No matter the bread, it’s all about creating the perfect bite.
